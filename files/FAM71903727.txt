Material fatigue-resistant optimization design method based on machine learning
The invention discloses a material fatigue resistance optimization design method based on machine learning, which is characterized in that correlation and influence relation analysis among data is carried out based on a machine learning theory, material fatigue resistance and structural design optimization are carried out from the perspective of the data, and original test data required by machine learning is obtained through a design test; then, analyzing the test process based on finite elements to obtain various detailed data of the test process, and enriching the data types and the number; and finally, constructing a machine learning algorithm suitable for the anti-fatigue design based on the input and output data types of the test and calculation, and finally applying the machine learning algorithm to the anti-fatigue design and structural design optimization of the material. Therefore, the method provided by the invention can meet the engineering application requirements of material fatigue resistance and structure optimization design.
