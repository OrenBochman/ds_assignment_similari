A machine learning method for multivalued function optimization problems
A machine learning method for multivalued function optimization problems, including: constructing a mathematical expression of a multivalued function; According to the mathematical expression, the speed update formula and the position update formula of the improved particle swarm optimization algorithm are used to calculate and generate a minimized multivalued function, and according to the minimized multivalued function, the local optimum and the optimal solution are obtained from the plurality of candidate solutions.
